Prerequisites:
* Java SE 8
* Python 2.7
	* flask
	* requests

1. Launch MainPiServer, preferably with run_system.bat. This will launch both the MainPiServer and the Minecraft server.
2. Launch HomePiServer, preferably with run_homepi.bat Substitute <homePiAddress> and <mainPiAddress> with the appropriate IP-addresses.
3. Launch PicKnicK (the Android-application) and make a request to the MainPiServer by using the menu button on your Android phone.
